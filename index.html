What we do

ClearFrame Advisory works upstream of implementation. We support leadership teams in making business-first decisions on AI transformation by assessing necessity, proportionality, and long-term impact before commitments harden.

AI transformation suitability assessment

Independent evaluation of whether AI should be introduced into a given process, and what organisational and operational changes this would entail.

Decision rationale & trade-offs

Clear articulation of why AI is adopted, limited, or avoided—making decisions understandable and defensible over time.

Senior stakeholder alignment

Focused discussions to align business, risk, and delivery perspectives before momentum narrows the decision space.

How we assess

Assessments are grounded in business reality rather than technological enthusiasm. The focus is on whether AI improves the process without eroding accountability, trust, or decision quality.

  • Clarity of the business objective
  • Impact and reversibility of the change
  • Accountability and ownership
  • Explainability and stakeholder trust
  • Operational constraints and failure modes
